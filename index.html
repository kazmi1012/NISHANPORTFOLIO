About Me

I'm Nishan Gurung, a dedicated student currently pursuing my Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) at Texas International College. My passion for creating exceptional digital experiences led me to complete comprehensive UI/UX training at Mind Riser Institute.

With a strong foundation in both design and development, I specialize in creating intuitive user interfaces and engaging web experiences. My educational background, combined with specialized training, gives me a unique perspective on how technology and design work together to solve real-world problems.

I'm committed to staying current with the latest design trends and development technologies, always striving to create solutions that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and user-friendly.
